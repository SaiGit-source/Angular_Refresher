Angular Forms

Two approaches: Template-driven forms, Reactive forms
For demo, we will focus on Reactive forms. Preferred for more complex scenarios. We get explicit control over data flow and validation. 

Template-driven forms are built using Angular derivatives directly in the HTML Template. Great for simple forms, however, they hide some of the magic behind the scenes

Reactive forms are defined and managed entirely in TypeScript. It controls more control of form's data and validation rules
